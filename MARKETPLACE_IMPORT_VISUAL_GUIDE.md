## Color Scheme

### Entity Type Colors

- **Rulebooks (RB)**: `#667eea` (Purple)
- **Rules (R)**: `#48bb78` (Green)
- **Prompt Sections (PS)**: `#ed8936` (Orange)
- **Datatypes (DT)**: `#38b2ac` (Teal)

### Action Colors

- **Import Button**: `#48bb78` (Green)
- **Download Button**: `#667eea` (Purple outline)
- **Already Imported**: `#e2e8f0` (Gray)
- **Selected Card**: `#667eea` (Purple border)
